CAMCA AIxMedicine Biweekly Seminar

Scope and Objective

We at CAMCA is a group of passionate people with background in biomedical engineering, data science, artificial intelligence, and clinical practice. Our vision for this seminar is to facilitate smart healthcare delivery by bridging the expertise of clinical experts into cutting-edge AI systems, with a focus on the integration between AI and medical/biomedical applications.
